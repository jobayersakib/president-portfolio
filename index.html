About The President

Jobayer Ahmed Sakib, also known as Jobayer JUBO, is a dynamic leader with a proven track record in leading teams, managing high-impact projects, and fostering growth through strategic initiatives. With over four years of corporate experience, Jobayer has demonstrated exceptional skills in project management, event coordination, and organizational development. Jobayer Ahmed Sakib began his academic journey at Banani Bidyaniketan School & College (BBNSC) and later continued at Bangladesh Navy College, where he excelled in the Science stream, achieving a GPA of 5.0 in both SSC and HSC. He then pursued a B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering from Northern University Bangladesh (NUB), where he developed a strong foundation in technology and problem-solving. Currently, he is furthering his studies at AIUB, specializing in advanced topics in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science to expand his expertise and career prospects. As the President of the NUB Computer Club, he successfully organized a wide range of events, including programming contests, knowledge-sharing sessions, and large-scale cultural programs. His strategic leadership contributed to the club’s success in providing essential courses and opportunities for professional development to students, helping them hone their technical and soft skills. In his role as an Executive in Business Development at South Heaven Architects, Jobayer excelled in overseeing HR functions, financial management, and client relationships, ensuring smooth operations and maintaining long-term partnerships. His expertise in banking procedures, documentation, and project coordination has contributed significantly to the company's operational efficiency. Jobayer’s leadership extends to community-focused initiatives, where he has coordinated multiple charitable projects, such as fundraising for flood victims and managing traffic control at the airport, demonstrating his commitment to making a positive impact beyond the corporate environment. With a strong foundation in communication, problem-solving, and team management, Jobayer continues to inspire and lead by example, proving himself as a valuable asset in any organization.
